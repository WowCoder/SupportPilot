How do I create my first support ticket? Getting Started ▼

To create your first ticket, click the New Ticket button on your dashboard or navigate to Messages and click the compose button. Fill in the subject, describe your issue in detail, select a priority level, and attach any relevant screenshots or log files. The support team typically responds within 2 hours for business-hours tickets.

What is the typical response time for support tickets? Getting Started ▼

Standard response times depend on your plan: Enterprise — 30 minutes (24/7), Business — 2 hours (business hours), Starter — 8 hours (business hours). Critical and High priority tickets are always prioritized, regardless of plan level.

How do I update my billing information? Billing ▼

Navigate to Settings > Billing from the sidebar. You can update your credit card, switch between monthly and annual billing, view past invoices, and update your billing address. Changes take effect immediately. For enterprise invoicing, contact your account manager.

How do I set up SSO for my organization? Security ▼

SSO setup is available on Business and Enterprise plans. Go to Settings > Security > Single Sign-On. We support SAML 2.0 (Okta, Azure AD, OneLogin) and OpenID Connect. You'll need admin access to your identity provider to complete the configuration. Our support team can assist with the setup — just open a ticket with the subject "SSO Setup".

Which third-party integrations are supported? Integrations ▼

SupportPilot integrates with Slack (ticket creation + notifications), Jira (bi-directional sync), GitHub (issue linking), Zendesk (migration importer), Salesforce (case sync), and Microsoft Teams. All integrations are configured from Settings > Integrations. API access is also available on Enterprise plans for custom integrations.

Why am I not receiving email notifications? Troubleshooting ▼

First, check your Settings > Notifications to ensure email alerts are enabled for the events you want. Then check your spam/junk folder — add notifications@supportpilot.com to your contacts. If you're still not receiving emails, your company's mail server may be blocking our domain. Contact your IT team or open a ticket and we'll help troubleshoot.

The dashboard is loading slowly — what can I do? Troubleshooting ▼

Dashboard performance can slow down with very large datasets (10,000+ tickets). Try: (1) applying date range filters to narrow the view, (2) clearing your browser cache, (3) checking your network connection to our servers at status.supportpilot.com. If the issue persists, open a ticket with your browser + OS version and we'll investigate.
